Ariana Grande Kendall Jenner Bella Hadid
Without a question, Ariana has a playful, lively, and feminine style. She enjoys wearing both dresses and skirts as such. Some of her favourite dresses are fit and flares, which have a fitted bodice and a flared skirt. Kendall Jenner's style is fashionable with a focus on upgraded fundamentals. In particular for evening outfits, she is not hesitant to go all out with a new trend. However, she dresses extremely casually and sports a traditional model-off-duty appearance during the day. She likes to mix classic menswear pieces with sheer tops, vintage finds, and even a few throwbacks from the 2000s. Bella exudes an attraction with her street style that will have you coveting everything from small sunglasses to Ugg boots and cargo pants.
